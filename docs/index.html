HA Voice

Home Assistant Voice Satellite for rooted LG webOS TVs

Use your Magic Remote mic button to control Home Assistant by voice. Supports LG ThinQ AI STT (fast, on-device) and Home Assistant Whisper, with intent recognition and TTS playback.

rootRequired Requires Homebrew Channel with elevated service access.

Add to Homebrew Channel

  1. Open Homebrew Channel on your TV
  2. Go to Settings → Repositories
  3. Add the URL below as a custom repository:
https://lorek123.github.io/lg-ha-voice/api/apps.json

Tap the URL to select all, then copy it.

Prerequisites

Mic button interception requires org.webosbrew.inputhook installed on the TV.

Note The changes needed to support the mic button (keycode 428) may not yet be merged into the upstream lginputhook repository.
